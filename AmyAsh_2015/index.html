Association of Accounting Technicians (AAT)

Lead UI/UX Designer & Developer

Lead UX/UI designer leading a front end team of four developers implementing new products and services for the UK’s leading accountancy qualification and professional body. Engaged on initial three month contract, continually extended over a period of two years. Converted a static Drupal site to a responsive, enterprise level site with a user base of over 1.5m, using Bootstrap theme and SASS. Introduced new design processes of interactive wireframing from psd to development. Introduced a component-based approach to development team and built a concise component library. Set up regular meetings with the Brand Communications department to liaise with the internal print design team. Co-ordinated external consultancy for user testing, whilst implementing in-house split testing for the first time.

Extra-curricular

National Theatre Scenic artistry - Wonder.Land

wonder.land is a new musical inspired by Lewis Carroll’s iconic Alice in Wonderland, directed by the National Theatre’s Director, Rufus Norriswith and music by Damon Albarn. I was employed by the National Theatre team to create stunning graffiti that glows under UV light on the showstopping Caterpillar costume.

Michael Billington reviewed the play in the Guardian newspaper and gave my work a glowing review:
"The most eye catching [costume] is that of the caterpillar, with its unending train of green globules"
Wonder.land is currently showing at the National theatre.
